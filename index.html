Introduction

Kingdom Hearts is a video game series developed and published by Square Enix. The series is a collaboration between Disney Interactive Studios and Square Enix, and it features charac ters and settings from both companies. The story follows Sora, a young boy who wields the Keyblade and his adventures to find his friends. The first game in the series was released in 2002, and since then, there have been multiple sequels and spin-offs.
