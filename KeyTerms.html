Key Terms
Here are some key terms that are commonly used in the Kingdom Hearts series:
Keyblade A powerful weapon that can be wielded by certain individuals. It is used to fight against the Heartless and other enemies.
Heartless Creatures that are born from the darkness in people's hearts. They are the main antagonists of the series.
Seven Princesses of Heart A group of princesses who are the key to Kingdom Hearts.
Kingdom Hearts A realm of ultimate power that is the main focus of the series. It is said to be the source of all hearts and the key to unlocking the secrets of the universe.
Traverse Town A world that serves as a hub for characters who have lost their homes to the darkness. It is the first world that Sora visits after being separated from his friends.
Hollow Bastion A world that serves as the main setting for the first game. It is a castle that has been consumed by darkness and is home to many of the series' villains.
