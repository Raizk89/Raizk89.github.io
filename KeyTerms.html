Key Terms
Here are some key terms that are commonly used in the Kingdom Hearts series:
Keyblade A powerful weapon that can be wielded by certain individuals. It is used to fight against the Heartless and other enemies.
Heartless Creatures that are born from the darkness in people's hearts. They are the main antagonists of the series.
Nobodies Beings that are created when a person with a strong heart becomes a Heartless. They are often more powerful than their original selves.
Unversed Creatures that are born from negative emotions. They are the main antagonists of Kingdom Hearts Birth by Sleep.
Keyblade War A legendary battle that took place in the distant past between Keyblade wielders. It had a significant impact on the world of Kingdom Hearts.
