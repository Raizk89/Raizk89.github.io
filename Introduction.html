Introduction

Kingdom Hearts is a video game series developed and published by Square Enix. The series is a collaboration between Disney Interactive Studios and Square Enix, The story of Kingdom Hearts is a massive and complex one that spans multiple games and timelines. The series began in 2002 with the release of the first game, Kingdom Hearts, for the PlayStation 2. Over the years, the series has expanded to include multiple sequels and spin-offs, each adding new characters, worlds, and storylines to the overall narrative. This website is a growing page and only contains information about the first game in the series, but more information about the other games will be added in future updates. This site is intended to provide a comprehensive overview of the story of Kingdom Hearts, including the main plot points, characters, and themes that are present throughout the series. The story of Kingdom Hearts is a complex and emotional one that explores themes of friendship, love, and the battle between light and darkness. It is a story that has captivated fans for years and continues to be a beloved part of the gaming community.
