The Story So Far

Kingdom Hearts 1

The Kingdom Hearts series has a complex and intricate storyline that spans multiple games and media. The story follows Sora, Donald Duck, and Goofy as they travel through various worlds (mostly based on Disney). The story starts off on Destiny Islands, where Sora and his friends (Riku and Kairi) are living a peaceful life. They dream of leaving the island to go on adventures. However, one day, a mysterious storm hits the island, and Sora is separated from his friends while fending off dark shadows with a mysterious key. Eventually Sora is transported to another world, known as Traverse Town, where he meets Donald and Goofy. Sora learns that his world has been consumed by darkness, and that he is a chosen wielder of the Keyblade, a powerful weapon that can combat the darkness. Sora agrees to join Donald and Goofy on their quest to find King Mickey, who has gone missing while investigating the darkness. Along the way, they enounter other characters who's worlds have been consumed by darkness, and they must fight against the Heartless. As they travel through different worlds, they also encounter various villains that control the heartless and seek to use the power of the Keyblade to unlock the door to the world of Kingdom Hearts, a realm of ultimate power. As Sora,Donald, and Goofy continue thier journey, they uncover more about the nature of the darkness and the true plot of Maleficent. She is gathering the Seven Princesses of Heart, who are the only one with the power to unlock the door to Kingdom Hearts. Sora and his friends must protect the princesses and stop Malificent from achieveing her goal. Towards the end of the first game, Sora and company arrive at a world known as Hollow Bastion. This is where Sora reunites with Riku, who has been consumed by darkness and is working with Maleficent to achieve her goal. Sora and Riku have a final battle, and Sora emerges victorious. Eventually the true villain is revealed and is known as Ansem, and he has been manipulating the events up to this point from the shadows. Sora, Donald, and Goofy team up to fight Ansem, who has taken control of Riku's body. They emerge victorious, but Riku is lost in the World of Darkness. King Mickey makes an appearance at the last moment to try adn save Riku, but is also sealed in the World of Darkness. Sora and group is able to save the Seven Princesses, including Kairi. They then decide to continue thier journey together to find King Mickey and Riku.
