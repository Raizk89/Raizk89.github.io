Characters
Here are some of the main characters in the Kingdom Hearts series:
Sora The main protagonist of the series and wielder of the Keyblade. He is a happy go lucky kid that loves adventure and is always ready to help his friends.
Riku Sora's best friend and rival. He is also a Keyblade wielder, but he is more serious and reserved than Sora. He struggles with the darkness within him and at one point becomes an antagonist in the series.
Kairi Sora's childhood friend and one of the Seven Princesses of Heart. She is a kind and caring person who is always there for her friends. Her and Riku are the characters that Sora is closest to, and she is one of the main motivations for Sora's journey.
Donald Duck A loyal and brave companion of Sora. He is a powerful magician and is always ready to fight against the darkness. He is also a bit of a hothead and can be a bit of a comic relief at times.
Goofy Another loyal companion of Sora. He is a skilled fighter and is always ready to help his friends. He uses a shield as his weapon and is known for his clumsy and goofy personality, but he is also a very dependable friend.
King Mickey The king of Disney Castle and a powerful Keyblade wielder. He is a wise and noble character who is always ready to help Sora and his friends in their journey. He is also one of the main characters in the series and has a significant role in the story.
